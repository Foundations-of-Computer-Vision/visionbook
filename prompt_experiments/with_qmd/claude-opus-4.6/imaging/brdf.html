Bidirectional Reflectance Distribution Function

When light hits a surface, reflected intensity depends on the incoming direction p, surface normal n, and outgoing direction q.

ℓout = F(ℓin, n, λ, p, q)

Lambertian: ℓout = a · ℓin · (n · p)

Phong specular: ℓspec = ks · (r · q)α · ℓin
