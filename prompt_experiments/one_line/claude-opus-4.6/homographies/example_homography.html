Homography Transformation

A homography (projective transformation) maps points via a 3×3 matrix p' = H p in homogeneous coordinates.

  • Colinear points remain colinear
  • Straight lines stay straight
  • Angles are not preserved
  • Parallel lines may not stay parallel
