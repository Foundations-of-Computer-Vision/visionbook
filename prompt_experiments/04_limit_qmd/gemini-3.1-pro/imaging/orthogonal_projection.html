Orthographic Projection

Demonstrates how parallel rays map 3D coordinates (X, Y, Z) to 2D coordinates (x, y) on a projection plane, invariant of depth (Z).

Notice how the projected size remains exactly the same regardless of distance, which is the defining property of orthographic projection.

Guided Exploration:
  1. Rotate the camera (left-click + drag) to see the 3D relationship.
  2. Move the "Object Distance" slider. In orthographic projection, parallel rays preserve the (X, Y) scale entirely regardless of Z position (depth).
  3. The projection equations are: x = X, y = Y.
