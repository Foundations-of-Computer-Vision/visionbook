Homography Controls

Explore how moving the planar surface changes its 2D projection on the camera plane.

Concept: Homography of a Plane

A homography maps points from a 3D planar surface to a 2D camera plane. Because the scene is flat, there is a direct mathematical mapping (a 3x3 matrix) between the 2D coordinates on the plane (X, Y) and the projected image coordinates (x, y).
